Our Mission

At Evergreen Adult Family Care, we are committed to providing an exceptional standard of living and high-quality individualized care. Our small residential setting ensures your loved one receives undivided professional attention, safety, and deep emotional companionship.

We believe in celebrating the unique life story of each individual resident, fostering high independence where possible, and always offering unwavering assistance with daily tasks in a respectful, warm manner.

Why Choose a Family Home Setting?

Unlike massive corporate institutional facilities, our adult family home replicates the true comfort of a warm residential environment. It delivers significantly lower staff-to-resident ratios, homemade nutritious meals daily, and tight-knit social bonding.
